Phonverter - a converter between IPA and SAMPA transcriptions

Phonverter is an online converter between IPA and SAMPA transcriptions. You simply have to insert either a SAMPA transcription in the textbox above or an IPA transcription (which you can make with the help of PhonPad) in textbox below. The transcription will automatically be converted from IPA into SAMPA or from SAMPA into IPA. You will then be able to copy the text and paste it elsewhere (if you paste it in a Word document, you will probably have to select an adequate font, such as Doulos SIL, Charis SIL, DejaVu Sans, Lucida Sans Unicode or Gentium).
